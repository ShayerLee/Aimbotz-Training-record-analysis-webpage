If you want to import your previous records, please format your Excel file in the following order:

"训练时间"="Train Time";

"完成时间"="Finish Time";

"每分钟击杀"="Kills/Min";

"每秒击杀"="Kills/Sec";

"准确率"="Accuracy";

"急停成功率"="Stop Success Rate";

"急停击杀率"="Stop Kill Rate"
